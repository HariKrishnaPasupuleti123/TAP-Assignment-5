Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and cultural significance, often referred to as "the City of Light."

Location


Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto, Japan’s cultural heart, is a city of ancient temples, serene gardens, and traditional tea houses. Home to iconic sites like Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), and the Arashiyama Bamboo Forest, Kyoto offers a glimpse into Japan’s rich heritage. Whether strolling through historic streets or enjoying seasonal festivals, Kyoto’s timeless beauty captivates every traveler.

Location


NewYork, USA

New York City, the Big Apple, is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic landmarks and vibrant culture. From the towering Empire State Building to the dazzling lights of Times Square, the city offers endless attractions. Whether exploring Central Park, visiting the Statue of Liberty, or enjoying Broadway shows, NYC is a must-visit destination for travelers.

Location


New Delhi, INDIA

New Delhi, India’s capital, is a dynamic blend of history and modernity. Designed by British architects, it features iconic landmarks like India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Humayun’s Tomb. The city offers bustling markets, rich cuisine, and grand architecture, making it an essential stop for tourists.

Location


Sydney, AUSTRALIA

ustralia, the land of stunning contrasts, offers breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cities, and unique wildlife. From the iconic Sydney Opera House to the natural wonder of the Great Barrier Reef, it’s a paradise for explorers. Whether it’s the rugged Outback or pristine beaches, Australia’s beauty and adventure make it an unforgettable destination
